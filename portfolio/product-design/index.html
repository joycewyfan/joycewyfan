CMH Floor 11 Keychain

A custom keychain created for Claudette Millar Hall residences on Floor 11 at the University of Waterloo. The design combines playful visuals with a clean, modern aesthetic to represent student life and community. It was created as a small, meaningful keepsake for residents, capturing the unique culture of Floor 11 (Animal-Crossing Theme) through simple shapes, character-inspired elements, and approachable design.
