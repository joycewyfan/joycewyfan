UX/UI & Web Design

Focuses on creating digital experiences that are both visually appealing and easy to use.

  • UX (User Experience) design involves understanding user needs, organizing information, and shaping the flow of a website or app so it feels intuitive and enjoyable.
  • UI (User Interface) design focuses on the look and feel — colours, typography, buttons, layouts, and all the visual elements users interact with.

Together, UX/UI and web design ensure that digital products are functional, accessible, attractive, and provide a seamless experience from start to finish.
